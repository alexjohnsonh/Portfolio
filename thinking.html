Babble

2022

Babble is student magazine created using design thinking for a university project. We created this by empathising with students and understanding their need for a community platform. We chose our colour scheme by again asking our target audience (the students) what they preferred and what would draw them into picking up a copy. We included a section for every major and other quirky content that our target audience might enjoy. We prototyped and tested our magazine and got feedback from our target audience on what they would change. This is our final version of the magazine.
