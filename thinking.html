Cliffe's Garage

Cliffe's Garage is an innovative student market born from a university project utilising design thinking principles. Our team of six empathised with fellow students, recognising their desire for stronger community engagement at Whitecliffe. The result was a vibrant marketplace that fostered interaction through several key elements: a central interactive mural for student expression, complimentary refreshments to encourage socialising, and interdepartmental showcases highlighting student work. To ensure smooth traffic flow, we prototyped the layout using tape outlines. Despite a tight two-week planning window, the market's debut was a resounding success, effectively addressing the community's needs and creating a dynamic social hub on campus. This project demonstrated how design thinking can quickly transform ideas into impactful solutions, enhancing student life and campus culture. (Video created by lecturer Rebecca Stokes)
