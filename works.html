The Grand Budapest Hotel

2022

For a class project, I designed and animated a title sequence for the 2014 Wes Anderson film The Grand Budapest Hotel. Using Adobe Illustrator, I created stylised illustrations that capture the whimsical aesthetic of the movie. The music, "Canto at Gabelmeister's Peak" by Alexandre Desplat, is sourced from the film's original score and complements the visuals. The title sequence aims to set the stage for Anderson's quirky comedy-drama about the adventures of a legendary concierge at a famous European hotel.
