The Grand Budapest Hotel

2022

A title sequence I created for The Grand Budapest Hotel. This was a class project for the Bachelor of Design and Digital Media at Whitecliffe. Illustrations made on Adobe Illustrator. Music sourced from the film - "Canto at Gabelmeister's Peak" by Alexandre Desplat. Wes Anderson wrote and directed the 2014 comedy-drama film The Grand Budapest Hotel.
