TheWooddHouse

I created a brand guide for TheWooddHouse. TheWooddHouse stands as a family-operated native plant nursery business nestled in the heart of Whakatāne, located in the picturesque Eastern Bay of Plenty, Aotearoa (New Zealand). The very land upon which this enterprise thrives was acquired by Jacqui (Woodd) and Fred Hughes back in 1995. In a passionate and immediate response to their purchase, Jacqui (Woodd) Hughes lovingly blanketed the land with native flora. Tragically, we bid farewell to Jacqui in 2022. Throughout her entire life, and right up until the end, Jacqui played a pivotal role in the realms of arts and environmental advocacy within the vibrant Whakatāne community. In honour of Jacqui's enduring legacy, her children and grandchildren have united to establish TheWooddHouse, with the aim of perpetuating her vision and making an even more profound and positive impact on our cherished community.
