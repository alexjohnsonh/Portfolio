Upstream - Fish Passage Solutions

2022

Upstream is brand identity created for a class project. Thousands of man-made structures are currently causing fish to be unable to swim upstream. As we all know, some fish migrate upstream to reproduce. These structures are causing our New Zealand native fish species to rapidly decline. Without knowledge or awareness of this problem, our native fish species could one day go extinct. Upstream is a company focusing on raising awareness for our nation's fish passage problems and giving people cost-effective solutions to remediate fish passageways.
