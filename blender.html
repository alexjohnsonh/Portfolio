My Room - Blender Sequence

For a university project, I created a blender sequence inspired by my thoughtfully decorated bedroom. The diverse fabrics and materials in my room presented unique challenges, but I embraced them to produce a visually engaging output that captures the essence of my personal space. Through careful attention to detail and creative problem-solving, I successfully translated the textures, colors, and ambiance of my bedroom into a digital render that I am truly proud of.
